5 Noli Me Tangere Characters

  • Crisostomo Ibarra
    The main character of the novel. He is introduced as a young man who has come back home to the Philippines after spending several years in Europe for his education.
  • Don Rafael Ibarra
    This is the protagonist's father who, although was a good man, had died an unfair death before the events in the story even began. A character that we actually never meet in the story and only ever hear about.
  • Padre Damaso
    A Spanish priest that proves himself to be some kind of villain in the story time and time again. He believes that the Filipino people are inferior compared to the likes of himself and other Spaniards.
  • Don Santiago
    A rather well-off man who is more known as Kapitan Tiago. The novel starts with introducing this character and the dinner/gathering he holds at the beginning of its story.
  • Tenyente Guevarra
    The one who told Crisostomo Ibarra what had really happened to his father and the truth behind his death in detail. One of the characters we see from the start of the story at Kapitan Tiago's gathering.
